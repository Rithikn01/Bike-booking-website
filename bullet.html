Royal Enfield

BULLET 350

The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is the epitome of enduring legacy and rugged performance. Known for its iconic design and reliable performance, the Bullet 350 stands as a symbol of motorcycling heritage. Here's what makes the Bullet 350 special:

  • Engine: 346 cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled engine that delivers exceptional reliability.
  • Power Output: Generates 19.1 BHP at 5250 RPM for steady performance on all terrains.
  • Torque: Produces 28 Nm at 4000 RPM, ensuring strong pulling power.
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual gearbox for precise gear shifts.
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 13.5 liters, offering an extended range for long journeys.
  • Weight: A sturdy 186 kg, giving it unmatched road presence and stability.
  • Suspension: Telescopic front forks and twin gas-charged rear shock absorbers for a smooth ride.
  • Braking: Front disc brakes and rear drum brakes for balanced stopping power.

With its unmistakable thump and timeless design, the Bullet 350 is crafted for enthusiasts who value tradition and reliability. Whether you're cruising through scenic landscapes or making your daily commute, the Bullet 350 delivers an unforgettable riding experience.
