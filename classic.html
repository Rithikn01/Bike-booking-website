Royal Enfield

CLASSIC 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a classic motorcycle that blends timeless design with modern performance. Its robust build and reliable engine make it a favorite among riders who seek both style and substance. Here's what makes the Royal Enfield 350 stand out:

  • Engine: 349 cc, single-cylinder, air-oil cooled engine for a smooth and reliable ride.
  • Power Output: Generates 20.2 BHP at 6100 RPM for effortless cruising.
  • Torque: Produces 27 Nm at 4000 RPM, ensuring superior low-end performance.
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual gearbox for precise control.
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 13 liters, offering extended riding range.
  • Weight: A sturdy 195 kg for stability on the road.
  • Suspension: Telescopic front forks and twin rear shock absorbers for a comfortable ride.
  • Braking: Disc brakes with optional ABS for enhanced safety.

With its vintage aesthetics and state-of-the-art engineering, the Royal Enfield 350 is designed for riders who appreciate the finer things in life while seeking adventure on the road. Whether you're cruising through city streets or exploring scenic highways, this bike ensures a memorable journey every time.
