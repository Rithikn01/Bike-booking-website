CONTINENTAL GT 650

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a cafe racer that combines classic retro styling with modern performance. Designed for those who crave speed and aesthetics, this bike is a masterpiece of engineering and elegance. Here are the standout features of the Continental GT 650:

  • Engine: 648 cc, parallel-twin, air-oil cooled engine for a thrilling ride.
  • Power Output: Delivers 47 BHP at 7250 RPM for exhilarating performance.
  • Torque: Generates 52 Nm at 5250 RPM, ensuring strong mid-range power.
  • Transmission: 6-speed gearbox with a slipper clutch for smooth and effortless shifting.
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 12.5 liters, perfect for urban rides and weekend getaways.
  • Weight: 202 kg, providing unmatched stability at high speeds.
  • Suspension: 41mm telescopic front forks and twin rear shock absorbers for a balanced ride.
  • Braking: Front and rear disc brakes with dual-channel ABS for maximum safety.

The Continental GT 650 is a true cafe racer, designed for riders who love the thrill of open roads and crave a vintage-inspired motorcycle with modern capabilities. Whether you're taking it to the track or cruising on highways, the Continental GT 650 guarantees an unforgettable experience.
