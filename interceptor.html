Royal Enfield

INTERCEPTOR 650

The Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 is a retro-modern roadster that delivers an unmatched blend of classic styling and high-performance engineering. Perfect for riders seeking comfort, versatility, and power, the Interceptor 650 is built for all kinds of roads and adventures. Here’s what makes it special:

  • Engine: 648 cc, parallel-twin, air-oil cooled engine with a smooth power delivery.
  • Power Output: Produces 47 BHP at 7250 RPM for dynamic performance.
  • Torque: Offers 52 Nm at 5250 RPM for exceptional mid-range acceleration.
  • Transmission: 6-speed gearbox with a slip-assist clutch for effortless gear transitions.
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 13.7 liters, ensuring extended rides without frequent stops.
  • Weight: 202 kg, offering a stable and balanced ride.
  • Suspension: Telescopic front forks and twin coil-over rear shock absorbers for a smooth ride.
  • Braking: Dual disc brakes with dual-channel ABS for maximum safety.

The Interceptor 650 captures the essence of the classic roadster design while delivering an enjoyable and versatile riding experience. Whether you're commuting, touring, or cruising down scenic routes, the Interceptor 650 is your perfect companion.
